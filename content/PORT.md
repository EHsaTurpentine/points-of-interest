We got fake-married twice. The first time started with me crying alone in the tankless gas-fired shower at 2 o’clock in the morning until Whatever It Was o’clock however many hours later, I couldn’t even remotely tell you. There was a rape in the house and they were handling it, she said. “Going To The Cop Shop” was the phrase I heard Naw say at some point. 

72 hours later she was on an airplane with my parents, leaving it all frozen right where she’d left it in Idaho, boarding a floating city that I’ve never returned from.

We got fake-married the first time on a real Mexican beach with real Mexicans pushing gaudy sombreros and ponchos on us as we tried to stand on all that white sand lipping the Sea of Cortez. Literally, at the very tip end of the world. Something about the furthest extension of the continent but we all always mean just OUR worlds when we say THE world.

World minus a letter = word.

It was never lost on me the dress she came out of the public toilet draped in that day was completely black.
